About MonPack

MonPack is your reliable package and delivery management system at Monash University. Our platform is designed to streamline the process of sending, receiving, and tracking packages on campus.
Designed by Oskar Hosken for FIT2095.

Features

  • Easy addition of drivers and packages.
  • Clean listing of all drivers and packages.
  • Filtering of drivers per department.
  • Seamless deletion of drivers and packages.

Get Started

To get started with MonPack, simply sign up using your Monash University email address. Once registered, you can start managing your packages with ease.
